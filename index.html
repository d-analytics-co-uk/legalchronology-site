FAQ

Is this “AI for lawyers”?

It’s a done-for-you chronology service. We use automation internally, with human QA, and deliver a professional draft chronology for your review.

Do you make disclosure / relevance / privilege decisions?

No. We do not assess privilege or relevance, and we do not provide legal advice.

How do you handle documents and confidentiality?

Matters are processed in isolation. We aim to minimise access, retain only as required, and provide auditability appropriate for professional services.
