FAQ

Quick answers to the questions litigation teams tend to ask first.

Is this “AI for lawyers”?

No. This is a chronology production service. We focus on document organisation, structured extraction, and traceable outputs—without positioning it as legal AI.

Do you make disclosure / relevance / privilege decisions?

No. Those remain legal decisions for your team. We can flag ambiguities and gaps, but we don’t make legal determinations.

How do you handle documents and confidentiality?

Matter-level isolation, secure transfer, and a “no data reuse” approach. If you have a preferred transfer method, we can work to it.

How do we start?

Click Start a Matter, share basic details, and we’ll confirm the secure transfer route. You’ll receive a draft chronology to review.
